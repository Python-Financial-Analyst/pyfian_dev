flat_curve

flat_curve.py

Module for flat yield curve models. Includes FlatCurveLog (log/continuous rates) and FlatCurveAER (annual effective rates).

Classes

FlatCurveLog

FlatCurveLog represents a flat curve with continuously compounded (log) rates.

FlatCurveAER

FlatCurveAER represents a flat curve with annual effective rates (AER).
