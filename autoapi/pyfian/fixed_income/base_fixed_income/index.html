If a bond price is provided, it is added as a negative cash flow at the settlement date.

Calculate effective duration of the bond.

\[\text{Effective Duration} = -\frac{(P_{+} - P_{-})}{2 \cdot \epsilon \cdot P}\]

where:

  • \(P\) is the price of the bond

  • \(P_{+}\) is the price if yield increases by \(\epsilon\)

  • \(P_{-}\) is the price if yield decreases by \(\epsilon\)

  • \(\epsilon\) is a small change in yield

The times to payments are calculated from the settlement date to each payment date and need not be integer values.

Parameters:

  • yield_to_maturity (float, optional) ‚Äì Yield to maturity as a decimal. If not provided, will be calculated from price if given.

  • price (float, optional) ‚Äì Price of the bond. Used to estimate YTM if yield_to_maturity is not provided.

  • settlement_date (str or datetime-like, optional) ‚Äì Settlement date. Defaults to issue date.

  • adjust_to_business_days (bool, optional) ‚Äì Whether to adjust payment dates to business days. Defaults to value of self.adjust_to_business_days.

  • day_count_convention (str or DayCountBase, optional) ‚Äì Day count convention. Defaults to value of self.day_count_convention.

  • following_coupons_day_count (str or DayCountBase, optional) ‚Äì Day count convention for following coupons. Defaults to value of self.following_coupons_day_count.

  • yield_calculation_convention (str, optional) ‚Äì Yield calculation convention. Defaults to value of self.yield_calculation_convention.

Returns:

duration ‚Äì Effective duration in years.

Return type:

float

Examples

Calculate the Z-spread of the bond relative to a benchmark yield curve.

The Z-spread is the constant spread that, when added to the benchmark yield curve, makes the present value of the bond‚Äôs cash flows equal to its market price.

The benchmark curve must be provided.

The Z-spread is calculated by solving the equation:

\[P = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{C_t}{(1 + YTM + Z)^{(t+1)}}\]

where:

  • \(P\) is the price of the bond

  • \(C_t\) is the cash flow at time \(t\), where \(t\) is the time in years from the settlement date

  • \(YTM\) is the yield to maturity

  • \(T\) is the total number of periods

  • \(Z\) is the Z-spread

The times to payments are calculated from the settlement date to each payment date and need not be integer values.

Parameters:

  • benchmark_curve (YieldCurveBase, optional) ‚Äì The benchmark yield curve to use for the Z-spread calculation.

  • yield_to_maturity (float, optional) ‚Äì Yield to maturity of the bond. Used to estimate YTM if not set.

  • price (float, optional) ‚Äì Price of the bond. Used to estimate YTM if not set.

  • settlement_date (str or datetime-like, optional) ‚Äì Settlement date. Defaults to issue date.

  • adjust_to_business_days (bool, optional) ‚Äì Whether to adjust payment dates to business days. Defaults to value of self.adjust_to_business_days.

  • day_count_convention (str or DayCountBase, optional) ‚Äì Day count convention. Defaults to value of self.day_count_convention.

  • following_coupons_day_count (str or DayCountBase, optional) ‚Äì Day count convention for following coupons. Defaults to value of self.following_coupons_day_count.

  • yield_calculation_convention (str, optional) ‚Äì Yield calculation convention. Defaults to value of self.yield_calculation_convention.
