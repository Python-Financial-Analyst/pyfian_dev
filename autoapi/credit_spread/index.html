Bases: CreditSpreadCurveBase

CreditSpreadCurve represents a curve of credit spreads (in decimals) at different maturities.

Parameters:

  • curve_date (str or datetime-like) – Date of the curve.

  • benchmark_curve (YieldCurveBase, optional) – The benchmark yield curve to compare against (e.g., risk-free curve).

  • bonds (list[FixedRateBullet] or tuple[FixedRateBullet], optional) – List or tuple of FixedRateBullet bond objects to bootstrap the spread curve from.

  • spreads (dict[float, float], optional) – Dictionary mapping maturities (in years) to credit spreads (as decimals).

  • day_count_convention (str or DayCountBase, optional) – Day count convention to use (default is “actual/365”).

  • yield_calculation_convention (str, optional) – Yield calculation convention to use (default is “Annual”). Supported: “Annual”, “BEY”, “Continuous”.
